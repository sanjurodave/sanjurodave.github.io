Welcome to the Catholic Church of the Visitation, Taynuilt.

Mass Time: 12.30pm

The church of the visitation is located in the village of Taynuilt in Argyll.
We are a small but vibrant parish and you are welcome to join us in the celebration of mass and for private prayer.

Location

The church is easily found, take the turning off the A85 into the village of Taynuilt, follow the road through the village, after crossing the river the road curves to the right, take the turning on the left and you will see the church which is on the right.
